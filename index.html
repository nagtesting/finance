Start Early, Retire Rich

Rule of 72

Divide 72 by your return rate to find doubling time. At 12% return, your money doubles every 6 years.

Rupee Cost Averaging

SIPs buy more units when markets fall and fewer when high, automatically lowering average cost over time — eliminating timing risk.

USA — Federal Tax 2026

7 brackets: 10% → 37%. Standard deduction: $15,000 (single). 401(k) limit: $23,500/yr (tax-deferred). LTCG: 0%/15%/20% depending on income. No national sales tax.

🇬🇧

UK — Income Tax 2025/26

Personal allowance: £12,570. Basic: 20% (£12,571–£50,270). Higher: 40% (£50,271–£125,140). Additional: 45% above £125,140. ISA allowance: £20,000/yr tax-free.

🇩🇪

Germany — Einkommensteuer

Progressive: 0% → 45%. Solidarity surcharge: 5.5% on tax. Church tax: 8–9%. Capital gains: 25% flat (Abgeltungsteuer) + solidarity surcharge. Generous deductions for social security.

🇸🇬

Singapore — Low-Tax Hub

Progressive: 0–24%. No capital gains tax. No dividend withholding tax. Attractive for entrepreneurs. CPF mandatory savings at 20% (employee) + 17% (employer).
