Glossary & Definitions

SIP: Systematic Investment Plan. Uses Dollar/Rupee Cost Averaging to mitigate market volatility.

FIRE: Financial Independence, Retire Early. Reaching a corpus 25x your annual expenses.

Section 80C: Indian Tax Code allowing deductions up to ₹1.5 Lakhs for SIPs (ELSS) and Loan Principals.

Section 24(b): Deduction on Home Loan Interest up to ₹2 Lakhs per annum.
